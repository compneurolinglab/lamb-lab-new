Recent Conference Presentations

Our recent contributions to international conferences and academic meetings

Society for the Neurobiology of Language (SNL) 2024

Zhengwu, Chengcheng, Dr. Jixing Li

Brisbane, Australia | October 24-26, 2024

Two poster presentations: (1) "Modeling the trajectory of language processing in neural state space" by Zhengwu and (2) "Functional specificity in multimodal large language models and the human brain" by Chengcheng. Dr. Li organized the symposium "Modeling linguistic processes through experimental and naturalistic designs," bringing together researchers from computational neurolinguistics.

Annual Conference of the Cognitive Science Society (CogSci) 2024

Qian Wang, Dr. Jixing Li

Rotterdam, Netherlands | July 24-27, 2024

Presentation on "Computational modeling of semantic composition in sentence processing" examining how meaning is constructed incrementally during language comprehension using neural network models and brain imaging data. The work received significant attention for its novel approach to modeling compositional semantics.

International Conference on Computational Linguistics (COLING) 2024

Qimiao Qiu, Dr. Jixing Li

Torino, Italy | May 20-25, 2024

Paper presentation on "Multi-talker speech processing in naturalistic listening conditions" introducing the Le Petit Prince Multi-talker dataset and computational models for speech comprehension in complex acoustic environments. The dataset has been adopted by multiple research groups worldwide.

Organization for Human Brain Mapping (OHBM) 2024

Weiqiong Zhao, Dr. Jixing Li

Seoul, South Korea | June 23-27, 2024

Poster on "Cross-species comparative analysis of acoustic pattern processing" examining neural mechanisms of sequence learning across mammalian species. This work contributes to understanding the evolutionary foundations of human language capabilities and has implications for both neuroscience and artificial intelligence research.

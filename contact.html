Application Guidelines

Information for prospective students, postdocs, and visiting scholars

PhD Student Applications

We seek motivated students with strong backgrounds in linguistics, computer science, neuroscience, or related fields. Programming experience (Python, R) or neuroimaging data analysis experience is highly preferred. Please include your CV, research statement, and relevant experience when contacting us.

Apply Now

Postdoctoral Researchers

Seeking postdocs with expertise in computational neuroscience, natural language processing, or neuroimaging. Ideal candidates have publications in top-tier venues and experience with large-scale data analysis. Competitive salary and research support provided.

Submit Application

Visiting Scholars

We welcome visiting researchers for collaborative projects lasting 3-12 months. Our lab provides access to state-of-the-art facilities and ongoing research projects. Please propose specific research objectives and timeline in your application.

Propose Visit

Research Collaborations

We actively seek collaborations with researchers worldwide in computational neurolinguistics, AI research, and cognitive neuroscience. We're particularly interested in cross-institutional projects involving data sharing, joint publications, and grant applications.
